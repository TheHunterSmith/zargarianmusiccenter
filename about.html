Our story

A Glendale
institution, run by hand.

Zargarian Music Center is a Glendale neighborhood institution, owned and operated by Garen Zargarian for decades. We are an authorized dealer for KORG and Yamaha.

Whether you're a beginner picking up your first instrument or a professional in need of expert repair work, Garen brings hands-on expertise and personal care to every customer.

Family owned since 2005, the shop has built its reputation the old-fashioned way — through instruments that are well chosen, repairs that are done right, and a staff that actually knows what they're selling.
